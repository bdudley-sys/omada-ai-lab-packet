Lab 1: AI as Assistant 75 Minutes
"Treat AI like a bright, eager intern: Fast and confident, but weak on history, context, and consequences" [cite: 118-119].

Ground Rules [cite: 115]

You Own: Tone, judgment, commitments, and what gets sent.
AI Owns: Speed, first drafts, sorting, and structure.
Never: Use confidential names or sensitive details.

The Inbox

1. Manager: Update on new workflow by 4:00 PM today.
2. Peer Supervisor: Needs coverage for 3rd time this month.
3. HR Business Partner: Jordan's transfer request (Due Friday).
4. Partner Team: A rare "thank you" from a territorial supervisor.
5. Casey (Team): Frustrated, ready to escalate over schedule delay.

Workflow: It's broken. You're using a manual workaround your manager doesn't know exists[cite: 129].

Jordan: Their friend Alex is on the team and doesn't know about the transfer yet[cite: 130].

Casey: This request has sat for a week. They are done waiting[cite: 132].

The Prompt Framework (CTC) [cite: 124]

Context: [Who is AI? Who is audience?] Task: [What exactly do you want?] Constraints: [Tone? Length? Red lines?]
Lab 2: AI as Analyst 75 Minutes
"Treat AI like a smart, slightly careless junior analyst: Good at scanning files, weak at recognizing overclaiming" [cite: 260-261].

The Three Boundaries [cite: 269-271]

  1. Patterns are not proof: Do not claim causation from correlation.
  2. Interesting is not enough: Connect the pattern to a real decision.
  3. Explainability matters: Explain the chart in 2 sentences or don't use it.

Question Menu

Pick one starter to analyze first:

  • Pattern: Do people who eat more meals snack less, or both? [cite: 279]
  • Equity: Do snacking patterns differ more by race or income? [cite: 285]
  • Interaction: Does activity change the link between snacking and obesity? [cite: 288]
  • Fitness: Which single chart would you use to brief a senior leader? [cite: 292]
Analytical Frame: Race and income are social categories reflecting infrastructure and policy—not inherent group characteristics. If AI frames it as a "deficit," stop and reframe[cite: 258].
